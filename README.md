# Hospital Emergency Room (ER) Analytics Dashboard

## Project Objective
This interactive Excel dashboard analyzes Emergency Room performance metrics, patient demographics, and department workflow. It is designed to help hospital administrators monitor operational efficiency, identify operational bottlenecks (such as patient delays), and evaluate overall patient satisfaction.

## Key KPIs Tracked
* **Total Patients:** 479
* **Average Wait Time:** 34.90 minutes
* **Patient Satisfaction Score:** 5.30 / 10

## Key Operational Insights
1. **Patient Attend Status:** A significant operational bottleneck exists, with **57% of patients experiencing delays** in attendance versus 43% attended on time.
2. **Admission Demographics:** Splitting closely down the middle, **51% of patients were not admitted** (242 patients) while **49% required full admission** (237 patients).
3. **Department Referrals:** The vast majority of ER visits resulted in no specialized referral (263 patients), while **General Practice** (115) and **Orthopedics** (45) were the top two active referral departments.
4. **Age Distribution:** Patient volume peaks noticeably at opposite ends of the spectrum: infants/children aged **0-9** (70 patients) and older adults aged **60-69** (97 patients).
5. **Gender Wise Analysis:** ER utilization is highly balanced between genders, consisting of **51% Male** and **49% Female** patients.

## Technical Skills & Excel Features Used
* **Data Visualization:** Developed custom donut charts, pie charts, stylized KPI blocks, and conditional horizontal data bars inside tables.
* **Interactivity:** Implemented multi-tier **Slicers** for effortless filtering by Year (2023, 2024, 2025) and Month (January through December).
* **Data Presentation:** Combined area charts (underneath KPI cards) to display micro-trends over time alongside traditional distribution bar charts.
